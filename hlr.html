SFP / BO Decision Tree — Trinity HLR

Level breached → 1H candles set direction → 4H close confirms

Swing Low (Support)

Trigger
Price breaches below a 4H swing low. Evaluation starts on the 1H candle that contains the breach (C0).
1 Every 1H candle close — live status
1H candle closes… Status Meaning
Above the level SFP attempt Buyers reclaimed. Flips from BO if needed.
Below the level BO attempt Sellers holding. Flips from SFP if needed.
2 4H candle close — confirmation
Last attempt status 4H closes… Result Signal
SFP attempt Above the level SFP confirmed Long signal
BO attempt Below the level BO confirmed Level lost
3 What to do
SFP confirmed
Entry
Long at or just above the level
Stop-loss
Below the wick extreme
Thesis
Breach was a liquidity sweep, buyers absorbed it
BO confirmed
Entry
Wait for retest of level from below
Stop-loss
Above the old level
Thesis
Support broken, now resistance
